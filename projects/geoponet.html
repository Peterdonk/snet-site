PROJECT
DESCRIPTION

The Geoponet Cafe was established in November 2014 in Koforidua Ada in the Eastern region of Ghana and is aimed at providing affordable and easy access to the internet in all parts of the town. It started with ten (10) desktop clients and one (1) server that hosted the billing solution. In June 2016, when mobile phones and other handheld devices increased, there was a demand for wireless (Wi-Fi) by the people in the community for them to access the internet in their homes and browse comfortably. Step Network, with the help of cafe management, converted the cafe into a community internet hotspot. The project installed transmission devices in the community to provide wireless internet to members of the community. Primarily, the project seeks to broadly extend connectivity to Koforidua Ada.

our core
objectives

The primary objective of this project is to broadly extend internet connectivity to all of Koforidua and if possible, it's environs.

Objective 1

Dedicated Internet

Our very first objective was to provide a dedicated internet service to the people of Kuntu and with two of Ghana's most reliable internet providers as our backbone, we did just that.

Objective 2

Virtual Learning

Because we know learning doesn't stop when we leave our classrooms, our second objective was to provide schools in Kuntu with our virtual Learning Management System (LMS), to enable students continue to study even at home.
